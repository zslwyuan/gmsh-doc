11 /*
12  * MTrihedron
13  * A MTrihedron is a plane element composed of
14  * a quadrangle and two triangles.
15  * It serves as an interface between two non-conforming
16  * elements
17  *
18  * v
19  * ^
20  * |
21  * 3-----------2
22  * |'\ | |
23  * | '\ | |
24  * | +---- | --> u
25  * | '\ |
26  * | '\ |
27  * 0-----------1
28  *
29  */
30
